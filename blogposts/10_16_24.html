october 16th, 2024

medsszzzzz

alright ive given it some time. heres a little update about my meds and how i feel about them or something.

i would say that the medication has been working pretty well for me! ive definitely been more productive in recent times. though i must say that the amount of productivity isnt as much as i was expecting, but i really shouldnt complain. ill take any boost i can get as a win.

overall, ive been doing good. nothing negative so far which is great.

some other gunk

ive been working on getting my shit together with schoolwork and anything relating to school.

lots of old stuff to catch up on, lots of new stuff to keep up with.

tomorrow (or the day after) im heading out to my uncles place with my dad for uhm. not sure actually, i guess just to hang out and get away from the monotony of home.

funny, because i actually love staying home. i live in a pretty average suburban neighborhood and theres something about it i prefer over the midwestern countryside.

thats just me though, im sure theres people out there that dig the hell out of living out in the middle of nowhere.

i suppose i can see the appeal. its always nice and quiet and the scenery is very pretty.

i just cant get into it though, ive been enraptured by the amenities of a modern household, with a thermostat, clean and filtered water, and all that other jazz.

hmmm.... what else??? uhmmmm i dont know actually. i suppose thats all for tonight, thanks for stopping by.

a lot has happened in just the last year or two, and ive had a lot of time to reshape who i am and who i want to be. and i think i can say with confidence that im happy with where im at right now.

bye bye! for now at least!
